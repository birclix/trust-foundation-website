    OUR MISSION

    Mustafa Trust Foundation's mission is to improve the health and well-being of individuals affected by Thalassemia, Cancer, and other blood disorders by:

    * Providing moral and social support to patients, along with free consultations.
    * Offering free-of-cost treatment to patients suffering from Thalassemia, Hemophilia, and Blood Cancer.
    * Striving daily to extend and improve the quality of life for patients battling these conditions.
    * Promoting blood donation nationwide by raising awareness of its critical importance in sustaining the lives of these patients.

    OUR VISION

    Our vision is to provide 100% free-of-cost care to patients suffering from Thalassemia, Hemophilia, and other blood disorders in all over Pakistan and later on in Palestine. We are committed to reduce the prevalence of these diseases through widespread awareness campaigns, engaging communities through social, cultural, and medical initiatives.
