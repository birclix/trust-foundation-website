    THE CAUSE / PROBLEM

    Pakistan

    Pakistan with a population of approximateley 240 million, faces Overwhelming healthcare challenges, particularly in the management of thalassemia and cancer. With over 10 million carries of the thalassemia trait and around 5000+ new cases of cancer major diagnosed each year, the need for comprehensive healthcare support is urgent. unfortunateley, blood trasfusion remains the primary management method, as standarized tratment protocols are lacking. The burden on families espacially those from lower socioeconomic strata, i immense as they struggle to afford the ongoing care required for their children.
